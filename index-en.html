About The Company

Milaf Saudi Company for General Contracting, Construction, Communications, Electricity, Gas, and Water Installations was established on January 20, 1432 AH (corresponding to 2011 CE), though it began operations as a company in 1421 AH (2000 CE) by its founder, Engineer Abdulaziz bin Nayef bin Tuwaili. Its primary activity was general contracting, and it achieved a first-class classification. From its inception, it contributed to driving the country's economy and implementing the urban development plan through participation in numerous construction and infrastructure projects for various governmental, semi-governmental, and private sectors.

Our Vision

With its resources, capabilities, and experience, Milaf Saudi Company aspires to be among the top contracting companies in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia in the short term, in line with the goals of Vision 2030 and the promising investment opportunities it will offer in the construction and infrastructure sector, while maintaining its commitment to the highest standards of quality and excellence.

General Director's speech

Milaf Saudi Company was established as an institution in 1421H and later transformed on 20/1/1432H into a leading company, continuing its journey of serving clients while contributing to the development of the nation and supporting construction and infrastructure projects across the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, in alignment with Saudi Vision 2030 and during the prosperous era of the Custodian of the Two Holy Mosques, King Salman bin Abdulaziz Al Saud, and His Royal Highness Crown Prince Mohammed bin Salman bin Abdulaziz – may God preserve them.

Since our inception, we have placed serving the nation, our clients, and our partners at the heart of our priorities, driven by a clear vision founded on continuous development and keeping pace with the latest technologies in construction, building, and infrastructure, ensuring the delivery of our projects to the highest standards of quality and efficiency.

Believing that true excellence is built upon expertise and capability, we have remained committed to attracting top professional talent and building integrated technical and administrative teams led by elite engineers and specialists, ensuring outstanding performance and strengthening our leadership position.

At Milaf Saudi, we move confidently toward the future, striving to be a trusted partner in delivering exceptional projects while maintaining our unwavering commitment to quality, earning our clients’ trust, and achieving their satisfaction—principles that remain the foundation of our success and continued leadership.
